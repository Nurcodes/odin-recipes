Cali Burrito

Description

California Burrito originating in California, is a delicious meal a fresh tortilla wrap filled with carne asada, fries, pico, sour cream and guacemola

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Carne Asada
  • Fries
  • Pico
  • Sour Cream
  • Guacemola

Steps

  1. Warm Tortilla
  2. Add chopped beef
  3. Add cut fries
  4. Add condiments
